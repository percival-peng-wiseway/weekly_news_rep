Albanese Government to Legislate: Large Data Centres Must Become "Net Generators"
Energy-Storage.news · 2026-07-19 · Source
PM Albanese announced at the University of Sydney on Jul 15 that next-generation large-scale data centres will be legally required to build new renewable generation and storage, injecting no less than their consumption into the grid and bearing full connection costs. A new AI Office will be established within the Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet. The government aims to reach consensus with states at next month's National Cabinet and introduce legislation early next year.
